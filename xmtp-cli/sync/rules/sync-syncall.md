## Sync and syncall

**Sync conversations:**

```bash
xmtp sync
```

Syncs conversations and groups (incremental).

**Sync all conversations and messages:**

```bash
xmtp syncall
```

Syncs all conversations and their messages. Use when you need a full sync or after onboarding or reconnecting.
